Creation & idea :-


Established in mid-2023, 3 web development students decided to
start a project with the aim of developing a website that helps Med
students figure out which part in the medical field they want to specialize in and
provide accurate information and knowledge to help them continue with their medical journey.

Our vision :-

Becoming the most trusted and reliable source to provide all the information Medical students need.

Our goal :-

Provide our future doctors with all the knowledge and info they need to help them make their life changing decision and achieve their dreams.

Our team & developers :-

The team consists of 3 students that currently live in Amman Jordan and study at the Applied science private university :- Mohammad Al-Masri. Mohammad Abed. Murad Krishan.
